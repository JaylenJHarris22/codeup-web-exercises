Petrified Forest National Park

  • It is 221,390 acres
  • It has over 800 archeological and historic sites
  • The only national park that contains a segment of route 66
  • Was designated a National Park on December 9, 1962

Death Valley National Park

  • It is the lowest point in North America
  • Some of the rock move on their own
  • It is labeled the hottest place on earth
  • Upon some dunes the sand will make a sound that resembles music

Grand Canyon National Park

  • It is bigger than the state of Rhode Island
  • It is the most visited National Park in the United States
  • The most dangerous animal is the rock squirrel
  • You can get an aerial view without leaving the ground
